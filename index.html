🏆 1st Place

Won the BrainStorm 2026
BCI Hackathon

Our team took 1st place at the BrainStorm 2026 Track 1 hackathon hosted by Precision Neuroscience — a competition on real-time neural decoding from high-density ECoG brain recordings.

The challenge: build a causal, streaming ML model decoding auditory stimuli from 1024-channel brain signals in real-time, optimized simultaneously for accuracy, latency, and model size within a 25 MB footprint.

I designed QSimeonEMANet — a novel architecture combining exponential moving averages with Gumbel-Softmax learned channel selection, achieving top scores across all three competition metrics.
